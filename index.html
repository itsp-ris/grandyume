Dodgers vs. Yankees: Who's The Most Valuable Team In MLB?

Not most popular, but most valuable, though the more popular a team is, the more revenue is generated. This will be the parameter used to assess a team's worth.

The LA Dodgers topped the list based on accumulated attendance from 2016 (Figure I). The St. Louis Cardinals, New York Yankees, Chicago Cubs, and San Francisco Giants round out the list (Figure I). The top five teams change yearly, but one thing remains constant: the LA Dodgers are always at the top of the list (Figure II). Although it's estimated that 70% of their high attendance is due to their home stadium capacity, the clubs don't always play at home - it'd be ignorant to proclaim them the most valued team based on that number alone.

Besides tickets and concessions businesses, Forbes stated LA Dodgers contracted the highest local cable television rights fees in 2021, followed by NY Yankees. Both are among the top five for average viewership in the 2022 season. Citing these data, both teams have a sizable fanbase contributing to merchandise sales, endorsement sales, sponsorship appeals, etc., on top of the above. According to Statista, their (Dodgers & Yankees) revenue more than tripled in 2021, recouping losses suffered over the pandemic outbreak in the previous year.

Recently, Spectrum SportsNet signed a 25-year agreement deal worth $8.35 billion with the Dodgers for exclusive broadcast rights, while the Yankees acquired back for $3.47 billion and founded YES Network to air their exclusive games. These contracts added value to respective teams, making them more costly.

The Dodgers triumph when one just looks at revenue. However, their long history that has earned them the title of a blue-chip team is a vital asset that should not be overlooked. Part two of Dodgers vs. Yankees: Who's The Most Valuable Team will be covering it.
